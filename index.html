ABOUT US

Invite you to discover the true taste of Japan

A truly authentic Japanese fine dining experience located in the heart of Menteng, Jakarta. Our restaurant is dedicated to bringing you the best and most authentic Japanese cuisine, prepared by skilled and experienced chefs.

At Sushi Manna, we pride ourselves on our commitment to using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients in all of our dishes. From our carefully selected seafood to our hand-picked vegetables and herbs, every element of our cuisine is chosen with care and attention to detail.
